Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza is crispy on the outside, soft in the center, and topped with a delicious mix of tomato sauce, melted mozzarella, and your favorite toppings. It’s a perfect treat for pizza night, made fresh in your own kitchen.

You can customize your pizza with vegetables, meats, or even pineapple if you’re feeling adventurous. It's easy to make, fun to build, and tastes way better than store-bought!

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough base (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni or your choice of toppings
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps To Make Pizza!

  1. Preheat your oven to 220°C (430°F) and line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add your toppings.
  5. Drizzle olive oil and sprinkle oregano (and chili flakes if using) on top.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Top with fresh basil, slice, and serve hot!
