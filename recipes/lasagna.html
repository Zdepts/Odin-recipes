Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe layers perfectly seasoned meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and sheets of tender pasta, all topped with a generous amount of melted cheese. It's the ultimate comfort food, baked until bubbly and golden brown on top.

Great for feeding a crowd or meal prepping for the week, lasagna is both hearty and satisfying. You can adjust the ingredients with vegetables or use a vegetarian sauce for a lighter twist.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef or chicken
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps To Make Lasagna!

  1. Boil lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, sauté onions and garlic in olive oil. Add ground meat and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Add a layer of noodles, then ricotta, sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat the layers.
  5. Top with Parmesan cheese and a final layer of mozzarella.
  6. Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake 10 more minutes until golden.
  7. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
