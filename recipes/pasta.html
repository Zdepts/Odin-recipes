Pasta

Description

This pasta recipe features tender penne noodles coated in a rich, savory tomato sauce made with garlic, onions, and Italian herbs. Finished with a generous topping of grated cheese and a drizzle of olive oil, it's a simple yet comforting dish that’s perfect for lunch or dinner. The sauce clings to each piece of pasta, delivering bold flavor in every bite.

Whether you're cooking for family or just want a quick homemade meal, this classic pasta is easy to prepare and incredibly satisfying. You can customize it by adding vegetables like bell peppers or mushrooms, or even mix in some grilled chicken or sausage for extra protein. Serve hot with garlic bread and a sprinkle of fresh basil on top.

Ingredients

  • 200g penne pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps To Make Pasta!

  1. Boil water in a large pot, add a pinch of salt, and cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add minced garlic and cook for another 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the tomato puree and stir well. Add oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Let the sauce simmer for 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until fully coated.
  7. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese on top and garnish with fresh basil if desired.
